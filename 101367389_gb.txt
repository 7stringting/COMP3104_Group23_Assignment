George Brown offers a wide variety of diploma, degree, certificate, and postgraduate programs. These are spread across several schools, such as the School of Business, the School of Health Sciences, the School of Design, the School of Community Services, and the Centre for Hospitality and Culinary Arts, among others
The college has specific centres dedicated to specialized training and research. For example, the Chef School at the Centre for Hospitality and Culinary Arts is renowned for its culinary and baking programs.
